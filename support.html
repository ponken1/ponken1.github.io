Gridblitz — Support

Thanks for playing Gridblitz! For help, bug reports, or feedback, email us at 4i.user@proton.me and we’ll get back to you.

Quick answers

How do I play? Drag the pieces from the tray onto the 8×8 board. Fill a full row or column to clear it. Clear lines on back-to-back moves to build a combo for bigger scores.

What’s Blitz mode? You get 10 seconds per piece — place fast or the run ends. Classic mode has no timer.

How do I unlock skins? Some skins are bought with coins you earn each run; others unlock automatically when your best score passes a milestone.

Do I have to watch ads? No. The only ad is an optional one you can choose to watch to revive a single time after a game ends. The game is fully playable without it.

My scores aren’t on the leaderboard. Make sure you’re signed in to Game Center in iOS Settings, and that you have a network connection.
