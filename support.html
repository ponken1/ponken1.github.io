How can we help?

Answers to the most common questions about Gridblitz. If you don't find what you need, we're one email away.

Frequently asked

How do I play?

Drag the three pieces from the tray onto the 8×8 board. Fill a full row or column and it clears. Clear lines on back-to-back moves to build a combo for bigger scores. Pieces don't rotate — it's all about where you place them. The game ends when no piece fits.

What's the difference between the two modes?

Original has no timer — take your time and read the board. Blitz gives you ten seconds per piece, so place fast or the run ends. Each mode has its own leaderboard.

How do I unlock skins?

You earn the skill-tier skins by passing best-score milestones; a few others unlock by watching an optional rewarded ad. There's no in-game currency and nothing to buy — unlocks are tied to how you play.

Do I have to watch ads?

No. The only ad is an optional one you can choose to watch to revive a single time after a game ends. The game is fully playable without ever watching an ad.

My scores aren't showing on the leaderboard.

Make sure you're signed in to Game Center in Settings → Game Center, and that you have a network connection. Scores earned offline are submitted the next time you're online and signed in.

Will I lose my progress or unlocked skins?

Your best scores and unlocked skins are stored on your device. Deleting the app clears that local data. Leaderboard scores live with Game Center and stay tied to your Apple account.

Does Gridblitz cost anything?

No. Gridblitz is free, plays fully offline, and has no accounts or sign-ups.
