How it plays

Three pieces. One board.

Place the pieces where they fit. Fill a full row or column and it clears.

01

Drag pieces in

Three pieces wait in the tray. Drop each one where it fits on the 8×8 board. No rotation — just placement.

02

Complete a line

Fill a whole row or column and it clears, opening up space for your next move.

03

Keep the board open

Clear lines back-to-back to raise the multiplier. The game ends when no piece fits.

Two modes

Two ways to play.

Original

Untimed

No clock. Take your time and read the board.

Blitz

Timed

Ten seconds a piece. The timer drains on every move.
