Privacy Policy for Gridblitz

Last updated: June 1, 2026

Gridblitz (“the app”, “we”) is a block-placement puzzle game for iOS. This policy explains what information the app and the services it uses handle. We keep it simple because the app collects very little.

Information the app collects itself

None that identifies you. The app has no account, login, or sign-up. Your game progress, best scores, coins, and unlocked skins are stored only on your device (in standard iOS storage). We do not transmit that data to us or anyone else, and we cannot see it.

Game Center (leaderboards)

If you are signed in to Apple’s Game Center, your high scores are submitted to the Gridblitz leaderboards so you can compare with others. Game Center is an Apple service governed by Apple’s Privacy Policy. We only send a score value and the associated Game Center identifier Apple provides; we do not receive your Apple ID, name, or email.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The app offers a single optional rewarded ad — you may choose to watch it to continue (“revive”) after a game ends. The game is fully playable without ever watching an ad. These ads are provided by Google AdMob. To serve and measure ads, Google may collect and process information such as your device’s advertising identifier (IDFA), approximate/general location derived from IP, and ad-interaction data. With your permission, this may be used for personalized advertising.

Google’s handling of this data is described in How Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services and Google’s Privacy Policy.

App Tracking Transparency

Before any ad-related tracking, iOS asks for your permission via the standard “Allow Tracking?” prompt. If you decline, you can still play the game and earn revives; ads simply won’t be personalized. You can change this anytime in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking, and reset your advertising identifier in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking / Apple Advertising.

Children

Gridblitz is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The only data processing is the optional advertising described above.

Data we share / sell

We do not sell your personal data. The only third parties involved are Apple (Game Center) and Google (AdMob), as described above, for the sole purposes of running leaderboards and serving the optional ad.

Your choices

  • Decline tracking at the prompt, or turn it off in iOS Settings.
  • Reset or limit your advertising identifier in iOS Settings.
  • Delete the app to remove all locally stored game data.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the date above and post the new version at this URL.
